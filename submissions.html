SUBMISSIONS

stories@pmagazine.co

We invite contributors, photographers, models and artists from all over the world to send us beautiful unpublished material to consider for publication on our platforms.

Submissions

 Please submit your photos for our online stories by sending us an email with your story in low resolution. A large edit of 10-30 images is preferred. The photos of the story need to be exclusive for P Magazine, meaning they have to be unpublished. A minimum of 1000 px width is required for preview formats: .jpg, .pdf or Dropbox links (no Google Drive single images).

Email: stories@pmagazine.co

Selected Stories

Size: a minimum of 2000 x 2000 px width is required for final images. Format: .jpg, .tif, .zip, wetransfer or dropbox links (no Google Drive single images) Resolution: 300 dpi Color: sRGB – RGB (even if they are black/white) Credits: please include complete and final credits for staff, clothes and everything we should include. This should be written in a simple file: pdf, text edit, word, pages. Social Media: in the credits, include the Instagram usernames for photographer and model.

Guidelines

Our general approach is to create a timeless atmosphere, where seasons or trends are not as important, where the model’s look and personality, and the photographer’s work and style, can blossom and can last forever. We love all races, colors, genders, shapes and sizes, and we would happily review all the submissions we receive, but we will only select those that we think are aligned with our project.

We love simple, timeless & classic stories. The human body and its beauty is the main focus of our publication, aesthetic nudity is always welcome to express the artistic side of photography.

Models must have a natural look and they must be +18 years old. The story should fit the overall vibe and mood of our site, whilst keeping the personal style of the photographer. No vulgar or explicit pornography will be accepted.
